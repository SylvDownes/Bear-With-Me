Bear-With-Me

An interactive fun app to promote implicit learning for children

This is NOT the story of those 3 Bears but...

5 agent bears forming a taskforce with the purpose of discovering and reporting back information in their field of expertise. The Bear With Me app is a collection of dynamic character driven adventures aimed at children from the age of 5 to 8 years.
